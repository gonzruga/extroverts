ABOUT THIS PLATFORM AND HOW IT WORKS

This platform is for extroverts to connect by showcasing their favourite hobbies and activities. It works in three basic steps:

  1. Create your profile. (after a few hours you shall receive user code to access your profile)
  2. Add your favorite hobbies and Stebby activites.
  3. The platform shall recommend extroverts to you. It learns the more you accept recommendations

We are currently focusing on extroverts since they are more likely to be willing to:

  • share information about their passions.
  • meet new people.
  • host people

The main preposition and problem the platform is solving is that some people are hesistant to do something unless they know someone. is for extroverts to be able to host each other and/or provide information. The platform will recommend to you fellow extroverts worth connecting with. You can also state which type of activities you wish to try or be hosted. For example: "We recommend you host a South American designer who likes travelling and arts". "Would you be willing to try summer sailing in the sea? An Estonian banker is willing to host you".

Introverts are still welcome to join – in the future we shall add more diverse features for a wider audience. This is still a BETA version with work in progress. The design will be continuously improved. Your feedback is highly appreciated, particularly about the core functionalities of creating profiles, hobbies, stebbies, nudges and reviews.

Features Not Yet Included and/or Upcoming

User Login: we have currently disabled user login to make it easy to use the platform immediately. We shall enable it in due time.

Messaging: at the moment inter-user communication will be through private Nudge pages. Later we shall implement traditional messaging.

Images: adding pictures and other multi-media will be enabled in the future.
