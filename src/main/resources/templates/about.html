ABOUT THIS PLATFORM AND HOW IT WORKS

This platform is for extroverts to connect by listing their favourite hobbies and activities. They can request to be hosted. The platform also recommends people you can consider connecing with or hosting. The platform works in three basic steps:

  1. Create your private profile. (bookmark it or save user id. You shall receive via email within hours.)
  2. Add your favorite hobbies and Stebby activites.
  3. The platform shall recommend extroverts to you. It learns the more you accept recommendations

We are currently focusing on extroverts since they are more likely to be willing to:

  • share information about their passions.
  • meet new people.
  • host people

The main preposition and problem the platform is solving is that some people are hesistant to do something unless they know someone. The platform provides a chance for you to find one person to connect with so you are not a stranger. For people willing to help newbies engage in any activity, this platform is for you.

The platform will recommend to you fellow extroverts worth connecting with, for example: * "We recommend you host a South American designer who likes travelling and arts". * "Would you be willing to try summer sailing in the sea? An Estonian banker is willing to host people". If you accept to connect with someone, you will recieve via email a link to a private chat page. You can use the chat secret ID to access that chat anytime from the platform.

Introverts are still welcome to join – in the future we shall add more diverse features for a wider audience. This is still a BETA version with work in progress. The design will be continuously improved. Your feedback is highly appreciated, particularly about the core functionalities of creating profiles, hobbies, stebbies, nudges and reviews.

What is HOSTING?
  • Hosting is basically guiding someone. Providing information needed for the guest to engage in the respective activity. Hosting is being physically present or remotely available to provide any needed information and answer questions.
  • The host introduces the guest to people known by the host.
Features Not Yet Included and/or Upcoming

User Login: we have currently disabled user login to make it easy to use the platform immediately. We shall enable it in due time.

Messaging: at the moment inter-user communication will be through private chat board/pages. Later we shall implement traditional messaging.

Images: adding pictures and other multi-media will be enabled in the future.
