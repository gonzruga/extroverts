When adding a hobby to your profile, it is required to provide a relevant link. Overall, we want users to prove they are extroverts and we do our best to verify to some extent. We want you to provide a link that ideally could provide concrete information about the hobby and as a bonus might contain a picture of you engaging in that hobby. The link could be:

  • Website or social media of the company or community organizing the activity.
  • Website or social media of a company, community, group or individual related to that activity. For example, if you like a certain sport, you can provide website url link of your faourite team.
